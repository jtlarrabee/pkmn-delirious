Features:
    • Access an in-depth shop with tons of different ways to spend your Pokédollars!
    • Battle other players!
    • Catch Pokémon from Generation 1! (Other generations coming soon.)
    • Challenge players who are Gym Leaders, Elite Four members, and even the Pokémon Champion!
    • Constantly spawning Pokémon for each trainer involved!
    • Target specific Pokémon spawning by going to different locations!
    • Trade Pokémon with other players!
    • Various events and giveaways!

Commands:
    /battle [target] - Start a Pokémon battle.
    /challenge [champion | elitefour | gym] [number] - Challenge a gym leader, Elite Four member, or the Pokémon Champion.
    /ev [pokémon] - Check the EVs for the specified Pokémon.
    /iv [pokémon] - Check the IVs for the specified Pokémon.
    /pc - Open your PC, allowing you to view all of the Pokémon you have.
    /pokédex - Check which Pokémon you've caught.
    /release [pokémon] - Release the specified Pokémon.
    /shop - Access the shop.
        purchase [item] [amount] - Purchase the specified amount of the specified item.
        sell [item] [amount] - Sell the specified amount of the specified item.
    /starter - Claim your starter Pokémon. Only works once per player.
    /trade [target] - Start a trade.
    /travel [location] - Travel to the specified location.
    /use [item] [amount] [pokémon] - Use the specified amound of the specified item on the specified Pokémon.
